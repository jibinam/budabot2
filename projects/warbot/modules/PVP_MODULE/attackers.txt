To see attackers who are online:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attackers<end>

To see all attackers:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attackers all<end>

To add a player to the attackers list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attackers add 'player'<end>

To remove a player from the attackers list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attackers rem 'player'<end>
