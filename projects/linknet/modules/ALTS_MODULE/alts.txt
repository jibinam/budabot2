<header>::::: Alternative Char Commands :::::<end>


By registering your alts with your main, you allow the alts that are below the level lock (175) to register with the bot. 

*Please note, alts are NOT automatically registered. 

This means that each alt MUST REGISTER individually.

<highlight>Shows your own alts<end>
<tab><symbol>alts

<highlight>Adding alts to your list<end>
<tab><symbol>alts add 'insert name here'

<highlight>Remove alts from your list<end>
<tab><symbol>alts rem 'insert name here'

Removing an alt, will also unregister them automatically.
